The right light makes materials feel richer and rooms feel larger. Think in three layers:

1. **Ambient** — the base that softens harsh shadows.
2. **Task** — reading, kitchen work, or closets.
3. **Accent** — art, texture, or a feature wall.

Mix color temperatures with intent: too many competing tones in one view creates visual fatigue.
