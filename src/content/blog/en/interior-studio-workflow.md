A studio operates in three layers: **design**, **operations**, and **communication**. When we blend them without intent, the team spends its days firefighting.

## 1. Freeze scope by phase

Before purchasing furniture, write down what each phase includes—and what it does not. A short paragraph in the proposal prevents long debates later.

## 2. One source for pricing and status

If the budget lives in one sheet and purchase orders in another, duplicates appear. Centralize numbers and states (pending, ordered, delivered) in one place.

## 3. A weekly review rhythm

Block a short window to review active projects: risks, open purchases, and payments. Consistency beats a monthly marathon.
